My Story

I'm Dan Vogel, an ex-Google software engineer who has bicycled across the United States and Europe. Recently, I graduated from the Yale School of Management, where I joined a GovTech startup in the Yale Accelerator, wrote prolifically, and studied under Yale's thought leaders in financial and enterprise systems.

I enjoy the process of learning. I like being in tech because there is always something new, it's fast-paced and high-stakes, and I find this combination to be exciting.

Outside of work I enjoy leading my book club, being outside, and figuring out new songs on the guitar. I am always open to new projects that allow me to grow both as an engineer and as a person.
